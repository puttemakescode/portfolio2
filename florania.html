A protoype of an app created by me! This one is meant to keep up with your plants pace, download Florania, scan the plant that catches your eye and get the exact information needed! Find out what you need to keep your plant alive, where and how should i plant it and most importantly find out the name of different plants. This project was a fun one, something i wouldn't usually do in my spare time but I learned and had a lot of fun!
