Jag, En

This is a project I worked on for a school project at my old school. A redesign of the a bookcover by the name of Jag, En. Which is a story about a person who wakes up in a new body every day. I used this opportunity to show as much representation as possible, so if you take a closer look all the symbols in this one person is to show representation of some sort. A piece I'm very happy with! :)
